# RUNBOOK

## Use When
the task includes making edits, planning edits, validating risk, or explaining how a change should be verified and bounded.

## Workflow
1. Identify relevant validation commands before editing.\n2. Choose the smallest meaningful validation that proves correctness.\n3. Run safe validation where possible.\n4. Record what was and was not validated.\n5. Keep rollback or recovery considerations explicit when the change is operationally risky.
